有间 · Yǒujiān

I’m starting Yǒujiān, a software venture named for an idea in the Zhuangzi: complex structures have natural openings — 间 — and good work enters through them rather than by force.

Its first product, Soloist, is studio management for independent music teachers, built on one idea: the teacher’s own calendar is the source of truth. Not released yet.
